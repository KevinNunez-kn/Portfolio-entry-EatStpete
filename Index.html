About

Eat St Pete Food Tours is a leader in culinary and cultural experiences in St Petersburg Florida and was the first company
to offer a food tour experience in the city in 2012. Eat St Pete's first tour started in Down Town and was known as the
Original "SunShine City" Food Tour. The tours grew with the food scene that now expands into the EDGE district and
the Grand Central District in St Pete. The tours introduce both visitors and locals to off the beaten path eateries, the
colorful history of the city, iconic buildings both old and new; alongside hidden murals. All of our guides are intimately acquainted
with the neighborhoods and lead tour groups both large and small with knowledge and passion of the city. Join us for a
one-of-a-kind authentic St Petersburg, Florida experience today!
